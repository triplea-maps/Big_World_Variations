Variations of BigWorld.

1. June 1942, by Prussia, with a focus on implementing the new technology from v3, as well as national objectives, and new units.

2. Small's 1939, by Smallman, has many neutrals to consider taking over for extra income. Mod balanced for dice and ll, with good strategy variety. Has been around for years and balanced tested over 100 times, was edited from Nekahnets original 1939 version.

3. NekahNet's 1939, by NekahNet, with increased territory income and a focus on historical accuracy and balance.
